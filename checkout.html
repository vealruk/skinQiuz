What people often ask

What is Face Yoga?

Face yoga is a series of facial exercises intended to tone the muscles in the face. This is a non-injection method of maintaining facial beauty and reducing the signs of aging.

What is a personal program?

Your personalized program consists of exercises aimed at maintaining and re-energizing your skin to help it look fresher. It has been adjusted for your age and for your needs to follow your problem areas.

When will I see the results?

Results depend on how often you practice. It can vary from person to person. You can start from 2 minutes and increase it to 30 minutes per day. This way, you’ll definitely see and feel some results, even within two weeks. But for longer-lasting results, it can take up to 3 months.
